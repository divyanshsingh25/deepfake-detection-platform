About Our System

Project Overview

The Deepfake Detection Dashboard is a real-time forensic media analysis system designed for news organizations and content verifiers. It uses advanced AI to detect manipulated media through frame-level facial analysis.

How It Works

Our system employs a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on thousands of real and fake face images. When you upload media, the system:

  • Extracts frames from videos or processes images directly
  • Detects and crops facial regions using Haar Cascade
  • Analyzes each face for texture artifacts, compression inconsistencies, and GAN patterns
  • Aggregates predictions using robust statistical methods (median)
  • Generates a Truth Probability score with confidence breakdown

Key Features

🔒 Privacy-First

All processing happens locally. No data is sent to cloud servers or stored permanently.

🧠 Advanced AI

Limitations & Ethics

This system is a verification aid, not a replacement for human judgment. Results are probabilistic and may vary based on compression, lighting, and video quality. We prioritize transparency and ethical AI practices.
